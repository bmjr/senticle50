Data Rights

All tweets are sourced directly from the public Twitter timeline feed in accordance with Twitter's terms of service.

What is Sentiment Analysis?

To extrapolate opinion from a piece of text or corpora of texts is said to be the extraction of "Sentiment".

Sentiment can be around an emotion or regarding an entity, within Senticle50's analysis of the brexit debate both aspects are analysed.

Tweet Classification

All tweets within the dataset have undergone classification against:

  • Sentiment
  • Stance on Brexit
  • Stance on Brexit (Inclusive of Sentiment)
Neural Network Analysis

The Neural Networks used in this project utilised Keras API

Real Time

Tweets are collected, tokenized, analysed and visualised within real time
